Personal compositions

I am an intermediate level pianist and I have some silly little compositions that I have put together over the past. I have done some compositions for an escape room I used to work at, they didn't fit in quite right so they were never implemented. However, the songs exist all the same.

Music has been a part of my life for some time. As a child I always wanted to have an instrument, at the time it was drums. At some point in high school I finally got a piano. My dad got it for free from a friend and gave it to me. I didn't practice as much as I wish I had. I eventually started playing more as I got into college. I took classes to become better and I have been progressing steadily since.

Below is a list of several samples of the type of original music I have made. My piano is put away right now so these are just some pieces I had laying on my computer.

Music Title Composition Description
Darker type freestyle
This is something I recorded on Halloween while I was waiting for trick or treaters. It is a little jankey since I wasn't used to the key I was playing in.
Dungeon Smash
This was put together using a piece of software that allows one to compose 8-bit music.
Space Theme
This was originally intended to be the theme of the escape room I was working at. I wanted to encapsulate the wistfulness of nothingness.
Escape the Asteroids
This is another piece of the escape room compositions. This was to be used during a section of critical stress.
Finished with Space
