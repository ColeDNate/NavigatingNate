Writing

Creative Writing has been a part of my life since I was very young. Writing is one of my best skills.

Below is a list of poetry I have written.

Limitless Magnificence

With such beauty, serenity, and grace; it doesn’t matter
How far apart we are, it makes my heart start to race when I think
How your eyes shine with such a likeness to the sky
With skin as smooth and soft as the clouds themselves
Your smile makes you shine with such a radiance
None can contend, save for a full moon’s brilliance

A placid whisper upon my ear; the resemblance it bears
Is similar to a subtle summer breeze
A gentle kiss placed on the cheek; delicate as a drop of rain
Yet compassionate as the solace of spring
A tender embrace with her; it brings such euphoria
It will warm even in the harshest winter
A movement so marvelous; poise so magnificent
It can tell a tale during a silent autumn

Freedom of Choice

Words, sounds, intention
That which has meaning is worth attention
Distance, timing, emotion
Anything wrong causes separation

Dreams, freedom, adventure
Seeking the unknown for one’s pleasure
Cities, highways, nature
Traveling is always easier to endure

To think, to dream, to feel
Whatever it takes for this void to heal
By wing, by sail, by wheel
One must strive to make it real

Left, right, in between
Strain stresses at the seam
To touch, to smell, to see
Which will the right path be?

Choices, wrong or right
Only the end can tell if it’s right
Dawn, dusk, night
Embrace the result in the moon’s light
Let your Spirit take flight

Symphony of Silence

Within the melody of silence
Listening to the chorus of shadows
I can find the perfect solitude
As it acts as a brilliant song
A perfect rhythm without a sound
Another way to tell my story

This is my story
Created from the silence
A requiem without sound
Conducted from the shadows
The tale of my song
Within my solitude

Even in this solitude
I still have a story
But the only worthy song
Is the symphony of silence
Heard from the shadows
To listen, without a sound

There is too much sound
Making it difficult to find solitude
Having need to find a shadow
Somewhere to make my story
A place with total silence
At the very least, a quiet song

To listen to my song
And it's amorous sound
Sitting in solitude
Alone with my shadow
Reading my story
In complete silence

Without my silence
My soul is without a song
I can't create a story
Without that special sound
Alone with my shadow
My story of solitude

While I sit alone without a sound
Within my perfectly quiet solitude
There is no better melody than the silence
